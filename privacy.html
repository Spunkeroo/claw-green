Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Claw.Green ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website claw.green. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve your experience:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time on site, device type, browser, and referring URL via Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity.
  • Calculator Inputs: Data you enter into our carbon footprint calculator is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies for aggregated usage statistics.

3. How We Use Information

  • To improve site content and user experience
  • To analyze traffic patterns and popular content
  • To fix bugs and optimize performance

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4: For website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity: For heatmaps and session recordings. Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • GitHub Pages: For website hosting. GitHub Privacy Statement

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to the default retention policies of Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity. We do not store personal data on our own servers.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Use browser extensions to block analytics tracking
  • Request information about data we may hold (contact us below)

7. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
